# Business Objectives

## Objective
Digitize metro ticket booking using ServiceNow to provide a fast, accessible, and automated ticket booking system with QR code-based ticket generation.

## Objectives

### 1. Enhance Commuter Convenience
Implement user-friendly digital ticketing options such as mobile applications, QR code-based tickets, and WhatsApp booking to reduce waiting time and simplify the ticketing process.

### 2. Increase Operational Efficiency
Implement Automated Fare Collection (AFC) systems to reduce manual intervention, minimize errors, and lower operational costs.

### 3. Promote Digital Payment Adoption
Support digital payment methods including UPI, credit/debit cards, and mobile wallets to enable secure cashless transactions and improve revenue collection.

### 4. Facilitate Data-Driven Decision Making
Use ticketing and transaction data to analyze passenger behavior, identify peak travel hours, and optimize metro routes and operations.

### 5. Support Environmental Sustainability
Reduce paper consumption by replacing paper tickets with digital QR tickets, contributing to environmentally sustainable practices.
